About the Exhibition

Mikrobait brings together 25 contemporary artists exploring innovative artistic expressions through diverse mediums. Thanks to our generous sponsors, admission is completely free for all visitors.

Exploring Contemporary Art

Mikrobait challenges traditional artistic representations by presenting innovative works that range from immersive installations to digital media and traditional techniques. Our free admission policy ensures everyone can experience these thought-provoking works.

The exhibition runs from June 15 to September 30 at the Modern Art Gallery in downtown. We're open Tuesday through Sunday from 10am to 6pm, with extended hours until 9pm on Fridays for special programming.

Exhibition Highlights

"Ephemeral Structures"

Interactive

Gallery 3, East Wing

"Urban Perspectives"

Photography

Jamal Carter | Photography Series

Documenting 12 urban locations over 10 years to show the dramatic effects of urban change, with augmented reality elements.

Gallery 1, Main Hall

Sculpture
